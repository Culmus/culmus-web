Developers' Area

Preview Editions

This section contains early alpha versions of fonts which have not been yet released to the general public.
  • Frank Curled Lamed - font similar to Taamey Frank CLM, but with curled Lamed and without cantillation marks, in TrueType format. Designed by Yoram Gnat.
  • Comix No2 Ext CLM - font Comix No2, extended with Yiddish letters and diacritics, in Type1 format. Provided by Richard Schoeller.
  • Taamey Frank CLM with distinctive Dagesh Hazak and Shwa Na - font based on the standard Taamey Frank CLM in TrueType format. See also sample document and instructions for use. Designed by Yoram Gnat.

Legacy fonts

This section contains fonts which have been superseded for various reasons.
  • Hadasim YG - font similar to "Hadasim CLM" (available in main package) and shares character shapes with it. This font employs legacy diacritics positioning algorithm developed by Yoram Gnat, but due to maintenance issues the algorithm has been completely replaced in "Hadasim CLM". Designed by Yoram Gnat.

Garbage Can

Most items in this area were produced during the course of developement, but didn't make their way into the main release due to their low quality or lack of value.

  • Hasida - Hebrew monospace typeface (medium, medium-oblique, bold and bold-oblique), removed from the main release since version 0.71.
  • Kidma - an attempt to derive a Hebrew counterpart for the classic Avant Garde typeface. I didn't like the result and I don't know when I'm going to work on it again. License: GPL v2.
